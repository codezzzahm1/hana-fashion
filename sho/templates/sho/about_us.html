About Us – Hana Fashion

Hana Fashion brand was created in 2019 with a dream to empower women through beautiful and affordable fashion.

Hi, I’m Nihana, the heart behind Hana Fashion.

I’m a passionate woman determined to build my own business from scratch — and I’ve been struggling and growing since 2019.

I started my journey by reselling all types of women’s products, and soon discovered my talent in making handmade silk thread bangles. That’s how my craft page @bangles_by_hana was born — a space where you can still see all our dispatches, happy customer feedback, and behind-the-scenes work.

Over the years, I’ve earned the trust of many loyal customers who keep coming back for more.

I offer

  • Handpicked fashion accessories that are both affordable and stylish.
  • Customer Loyalty Points for regular buyers.

In 2024, I also started savings schemes for my customers — helping many women save regularly and shop smart.

By Allah’s grace, it's running successfully and gaining love and trust.

Now, I’m excited to launch this new chapter — my very own collection of hand-selected fashion accessories for women around the world, curated with love and care.

Thank you for supporting my small business and being a part of my journey.
